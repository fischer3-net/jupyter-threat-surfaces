# Module 00 — Orientation & Prerequisites

**Purpose**: Calibration. Establish notation, verify starting point, surface any gaps.

| Unit | Title | Time |
|---|---|---|
| [01](unit-01-vectors) | Vectors & Vector Spaces | 50–60 min |
| [02](unit-02-dot-and-cross-products) | Dot Products & Cross Products | 55–65 min |
| [03](unit-03-single-variable-review) | Single-Variable Review & Notation Setup | 45–55 min |

## Module Learning Objectives

- Confirm fluency with vectors and vector operations in $\R^2$ and $\R^3$
- Establish notation conventions used throughout the course
- Identify and address any gaps in single-variable calculus fluency

## Statistical Bridges in This Module

No statistical bridges — this is an orientation module. Connections begin in Module 01.
